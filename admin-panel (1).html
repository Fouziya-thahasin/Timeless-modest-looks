Track Your Page Views — Free

Google Analytics is a free tool that shows you exactly how many people visit your website, where they come from, and what they look at. All you need is a free Google account.

Step-by-step to get your Measurement ID:
1. Go to analytics.google.com and sign in with Google.
2. Click Admin (bottom left) → Create Property.
3. Enter your website name and URL, then finish setup.
4. Find your Measurement ID — it looks like G-XXXXXXXXXX
5. Copy and paste it in the box below, then click Save.
Always starts with "G-". Found in Google Analytics → Admin → Data Streams → your website.

Export Your Content

Download a content.json file. Upload it next to your website HTML and your website will automatically show all your images, folders, and affiliate links.

How to Use

  1. Click Download content.json above.
  2. Log in to your website hosting (Netlify, cPanel, etc.) and upload the file to the same folder as your main HTML file.
  3. Your website will now display all your images and affiliate links automatically.
  4. Every time you add or edit images here, download and re-upload the JSON to update your site.
